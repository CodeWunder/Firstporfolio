About Me

My name is Iwara Wisdom, I specialise in creating beautiful, usable, professional websites using best practice accessibility, latest web standards guidelines, resulting in semantic and seo friendly HTML and CSS. All my sites are lovingly hand coded.


Good design works to present information in such a way as to support the information it presents. When I design a website, I'm constantly considering how the site will be built and how it will function.

This way, you can be sure that both the design and usability will be considered along every step of the way.


I also love to play football, sing and write songs in my spare time.
